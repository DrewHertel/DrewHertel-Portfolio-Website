My story

A fine artist turned designer I aim to create functional designs without sacrificing aesthetics.

I've taken a winding road to UI/UX design. From printmaker, to climbing shoe cobbler, and most recently illustrator.

I've always looked for jobs where I could create and pursue my passions. Finding UI/UX felt full circle, creating a line through my past to my future. Shoe cobbling or Illustration may not appear to immediately translate to UI/UX design, but each enabled me to build a robust toolkit for solving creative problems.

Hobbies

In my spare time I enjoy getting outside to go climbing, seeing live music, traveling, and socializing with friends. That's not to say I don't enjoy a good day inside. There I pass the time reading, painting, playing video games, or watching movies.
